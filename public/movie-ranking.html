The Best Movies According to Kathryn

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

The Godfather (1972)

A gripping tale of family, power, and betrayal, it follows the Corleone crime family as they navigate the complexities of loyalty and legacy in post-war America.

Casablanca (1942)

Set against the backdrop of World War II, this romantic drama tells the story of an American expatriate who must choose between love and virtue when his former lover re-enters his life.

The Shawshank Redemption (1994)

A powerful tale of hope and friendship, it follows the resilience of Andy Dufresne, who forms an enduring bond with fellow inmate Red while seeking redemption during his unjust imprisonment.
